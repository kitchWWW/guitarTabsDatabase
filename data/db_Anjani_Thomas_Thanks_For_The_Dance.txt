      There are a few versions of this song on the internet.  This is a live version from the album
"Acordes Con Leonard Cohen".  Performed by Anjani Thomas.  Written by Leonard Cohen.

The song is done in the key of Ab, but if you want to tune down a semitone or put a capo on the first
fret, the chord positions may become a little easier.

It should also be noted that the song is in 3/4 time--like a waltz.

Note that the "F# G G#" is a single note walk-up played on the low E string.
All chords are played with a swinging cowboy rhythm where you play an alternating
bass line that is root then 5th. Even though my tab only shows you playing the top four notes of 
the chords, you should always finger the whole chord and let the accidental notes ring. I showed this as 
ghost notes.
I have included a small chord chart at the beginning showing that bass line for any beginners.
I am not good at tabbing rythms so that is on you to learn.
Suggestions welcome.
An amazing song and so fun to play.

                     POLICEDOG BLUES
                          By Blind Blake
                     Transcribed by MaxxDaddy

This is Blake's only song in open E tuning
Ry Cooder does a faithful rendition on his eponymous album, and
Jorma Kaukonen does a good version on "Splashdown" and "Quah". Cephas
and Wiggins do an interesting interpretation on "Guitar Man" and
Stephan Grossman has a few versions out.

Open E tuning: EBEG#BE (open D also works)

It's a fun song once you get the breaks tight. Play along with the
CD "Ragtimes Foremost Fingerpicker" to get the phrasing right. Any
comments, positive or negative should go to MaxxDaddy@aol.com.

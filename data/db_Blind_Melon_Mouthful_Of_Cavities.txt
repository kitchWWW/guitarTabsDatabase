Tab legend: 

h    hammer on
p    pull off
/    slide up
\    slide down
( )  optional note, in this tab it's an optional pulloff in the intro/verse
v    downstroke
^    upstroke
b    bend 

Examples: 12--b13b12 would mean that you hold the 12nd fret and after a little pause, you bend it up to the 
13th fret,then back down to the 12th. 11---b12p9 that you hold the 11th fret note and bend it to the 12th fret
after a pause, then pull it off to the 9th fret.

Dedication: This song speaks many unspoken words of mine,
involving a very special girl in my life. We only had a few days
together and we stayed up all night and day, counting our moments
together, trying not to realize our happiness would end. Leaving her
was the hardest thing I have ever done in my life. I keep replaying
every minute I have of her, hoping never to lose those memories.
Yet, they slowly start to fade...

But I will never forget her, and though we live
hundreds of miles apart now, I hope she's as happy as I am,
knowing that she's the one I daydream of during the day,
the one dream about at night, the one I...

...Love, Forever.

~~~~~~~~~~

to interpret, and to me, this is 101% accurate.

Hey everyone! Here's a cool song by French singer, Caroline Costa, called "Qui je suis" 
(which means "Who I am" in English).
Check it out: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lIf4XfgErfI

I did some translating and stuff, so here's an English-lyric version! (Scroll down for 
the original.)

Tuning: Whole-step down (DGCFAd) [If you don't want to tune your guitar down, though, 
you can put a capo on the third fret and play G, D, Em, and C.]

I'd suggest strumming in the chorus and finger-picking in the verses and ending.

Have fun! :)

Le tuning est de deux demi-étapes vers le bas. [Mais si vous ne voulez pas d'accorder 
votre guitare, vous pouvez mettre un capo sur la troisième frette et le jeu G, D, Em, et C.]
Je suggère grattant le refrain, puis le doigt-cueillette du verset et la fin.
(Désolé si mon français est terrible, parce que je suis Américain.)

Amusez-vous! :)

Cowboy Up
By Chris Ledoux
The "Ramblin' Fever" version

This is the version of Chris LeDoux's first 'Cowboy Up' the one you can't get on spotify.
It is a very easy song to play with only 3 Chords G, Cadd9, Dsus4 and the beauty with this is,
they can all be played with your ring and pinky finger locked on the b and e strings 3rd fret throughout.
This isn't 100% correct but I think it sounds awesome!

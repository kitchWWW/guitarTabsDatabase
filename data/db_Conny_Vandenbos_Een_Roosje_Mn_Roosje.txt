"Een Roosje m'n roosje" by Conny Vandenbos was released in 1974.
Song is translated "Daisy a day" of Jud Strunk (lyrics)
 and Gerrit den Braber (music & arrangements).
Song is in 3/4 beat and describes aging love and the loss of a dear friend.
Original Key is F#.

This 1975 song of Dutch singer Connie van den Bosch is in a 4/4 rhythm. The original
is at a fairly rapid 143 bpm. Personally I like it a bit slower than the original.

Capo @ fret 1, with these tabs, makes playing along with the original possible. No capo
will of course need transposing +1. You can of course transpose or use capo anywhere
to adjust to your own vocal range, loosing the possibility to play-along with the original.

N'Joy!!  Big M

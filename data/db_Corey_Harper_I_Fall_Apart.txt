This sounds reasonable with the original Post Malone audio version too. 
I tabbed the three basic styles used throughout: 1) simple picking, 2) general picking pattern & 3)strumming. 
You could choose to play one of the three styles throughout or use a combination of the strumming and 
picking as he does in the song. 
He does add the bottom open e string here and there, and does hammer on and off on the B string at times 
(at the end of some bars). Tabbing exactly what he does is challenging, as each bar starts off the same 
but almost always ends with just a slight difference in picking pattern.

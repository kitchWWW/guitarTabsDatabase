---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Written by: Stephen Stills
From: "Deja Vu" (1970)

Tabbed by: maguri
Tuning: Standard / optional: Drop-D

The original recording is played using an open tuning, so if you're looking 
for that, check out the tab version:
https://tabs.ultimate-guitar.com/tab/crosby_stills_nash_young/4_plus_20_tabs_1035751

This here is a chord sheet for strumming along. The key of original recording
is between E and Eb. I'd suggest playing in D and using a capo on the 2nd fret.
Of course, many subtle changes and embellishments are lost in this simple
reading, but it still offers interesting ways of expanding (see chords section).

Drop A tuning: each tab line is a section. Over the whole song is an A bass drone.
This song is structurally improvised and as such you'll be hard pressed to play it
exactly as Dylan plays it. It's all in 4/4 so he only really varies the 
addition of a bar or two of open chugging to bridge between sections.

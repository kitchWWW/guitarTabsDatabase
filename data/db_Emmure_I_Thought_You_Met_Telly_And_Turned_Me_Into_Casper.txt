Since no one has tabbed this, I decided to tab a somewhat correct one.
Also, you are going to need an octave pedal to go one octave higher for
that scream thing. And the amazing pick scrapes that the guitarists
do are by scraping downward an inch from the bridge/tailpiece.
Hopefully this is helpful to you. For timing, play along to the
song and figure it out. -Shawn

                         ALLES GUTE - Faber 
----------------------------------------------------------------- 

This song's chord sequence is pretty straight forward, consisting of Am, C, Em and D only. The strumming 
pattern will need some practice in fingerstyle, since the thumb is playing an alternating bass with 
hammer-ons, while the rest of the strings is strummed with the index finger. 

t = thumb pick
^ = downward strum with index finger
v = upward strum with undex finger

*Strum all chords only once and let them ring until the Am on gefällt; from here on downstrokes in 8-notes up 
until the last D of the verse. Mute this D on the fifth downstroke (the 'two and'). Then briefly return to the
picking pattern. The last two choruses are also strummed a little differently, just play along to the
recording and you will figure it out. The song then ends with the picking pattern and a strummed Em.

** On the last beat of the Am lift your middle and ringfinger and put your middle finger on the second fret of
the A-string; strum once and then go on with the c-chord.

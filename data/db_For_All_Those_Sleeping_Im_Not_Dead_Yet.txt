===========================================================================

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Ok, this is my first tab that I've made, but I've been using Ultimate Guitar for a while 
so i know roughly how a tab should be.

For some time now, after listening to this song, I've been wondering "How do you play 
song?". So I sat and waited for a tab so I could play it and, there is no tab. I was 
to myself "Someone hurry up and get
this tab online!", and still no tab.

So after a long time, I decided to try and learn how to play this on my own. I watched 
band do their acoustic version of I'm Not Dead Yet and after hard work, I figured out a 
to play it.

Now I know this isn't actualy the proper way that they play it, but it's a
way to play all of the song. Some of the part may be out on the tab if they
are just adapt them by listening to the song or e-mail me.

Eventualy I will come back, when I've learnt it, and write a proper tab with more detail 
both guitars. But until then, here's the first version.

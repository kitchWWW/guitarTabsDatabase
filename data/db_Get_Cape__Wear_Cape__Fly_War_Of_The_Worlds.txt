It seems quite accurate to me, but it's my first tab, so any comments or 
corrections are welcome, enjoy! 
(Thanks to Brand New for spotting the small mistake - fixed now!)
 
In the bridge and subsequent verse, when strumming the C C/B Am7, the C/B
chord is just there to help the transition from C to Am7 and so should be short. 

Chords used: C, C/B, Am7, Em, F , G
C/B = x20010 (I don't know if this is the real name for this chord) 
F=(1)03210
G=(3)x5430
/ = slide up

As far as I know the band plays this song using D standard tuning, but I wanted to make chords you can play
without having to tune your guitar into D standard. So we'll use Drop D tuning insted.
It's not as comfortable as playing in D standard, but will let us avoid all the tuning.
We'll use drop D to add that nice D bass note in the verse Dm chords and several other places.


In the beginning of the intro I suggest you play Gm as x55336. This way it sounds closer to the original.
In later parts of the song you can play Gm as you like.



THESE CHORDS ARE DROP D if you use D standard or E standart tuning adjust accordingly!

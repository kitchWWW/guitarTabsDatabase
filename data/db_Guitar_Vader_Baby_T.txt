Guitar Vader - "Baby T," Track 3 of their "From Dusk" album, 2001 -
also as heard in Jet Set Radio Future. 

Most of this song is Rhythm (Guitar 2) but the album version has a few extra parts (Guitar 1.)
A 3rd guitar part shows up near the end.

Guitar 1 is in the Right Channel, Guitar 2's in the Left Channel, Guitar 3 is in the right channel.

Since I don't know Japanese and Guitar Vader spoke little English,
the English lyrics included may not be quite accurate.

Whatever lyrics I understand are in quotation marks beneath the tab sections,
"like this."

Notes about how to play the song are in round brackets beneath the tab sections,

[Legend:]
=========

Above the tab:

... = Shown above sections that you need to palm mute. 

In the tab:

> = Play the first note in the next tab section.
h = Hammer on.
/ = Slide up. 
\ = Slide down.

* = Mute strings in that moment. 
x = Play a muted note.

PS (surrounding a section) = Pick Scrape. The slide inside this section shows from which fret
to slide down from, and which fret to slide down to. 
X = When two capital X letters surround a note, this is to show a section that you slide on
without fretting. (Only shows up at the end in this tab.) 

() = Brackets are put around a part that may or may not be in the song. These are "ghost notes."

Below the tab:

^ = Down strum.
V = Up Strum.

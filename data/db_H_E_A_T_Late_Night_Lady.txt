H.E.A.T - Late night Lady.

Notes: 

Intro: Play the E muted with pick and the E and D chords with fingers. The guitar in the record 
is clean, but in the live situation they uses overdrive, listen "Live in London" and "Live Sweden" to notice.

Solo: E minor. Pentantonic and minor natural escales. One guitar makes the intro, the other guitar prepares 
the solo with a little melodic line in E minor, play this line subtle, palm muted, stacatto feel.
Then the solo starts and you have to engage the wah pedal.

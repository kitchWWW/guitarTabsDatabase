Nots:
* You don't need to copy the playstyle of the song 100%,
  It's nice to play it your own way and experiment a bit.
* The cords are in roughly the correct position,
  Just follow the strum and it's timing and you should do alright.
* In the intro, hammer on the 'e' string on fret 3 while playing the 'D' cord. Listen to the song to hear 
  when and where, but it's pretty much every time (in the intro). However its optional.
* The optional 'G' cord in the chorous can be played by not holdiong any cord and strumming all strings.
  Not a particularly nice thing, but it sounds alright if you're not trying to be perfect. :)
* On the Lyrics 'Let's go', Mute the strings.
* I'm not 100% sure about the 'Am', this could be just an 'A' but I like it this way more.
* The reason the outro has two parts is because the strumming in part one is increasing in volume.
  And part two is played normaly.

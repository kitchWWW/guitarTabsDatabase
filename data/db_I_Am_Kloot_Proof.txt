Kloot afficionados will recognise this as one of the band's best loved tunes. 

They've recorded it twice - on 'I Am Kloot' (2003) and 'Sky At Night' (2010).

Unusually for IAK it uses a different tuning capo'd at fret 4, 
effectively [ch]A[/ch] [ch]Badd9[/ch]. 

I've seen this tabbed several times with standard tuning, 
but with respect to those efforts they do not reflect how 
the band / John Bramwell play it. 

It took several attempts for me to crack it, 
but I'm very confident this is a faithful representation of how 
the band play it.

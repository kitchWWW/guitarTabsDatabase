A simple strumming pattern is 1 2 + 3 +, however the real pattern is much more complicated
after giving the F a measure after the C chord, hammer on the middle finger on beat 1, leave it on for 2,  
and take it off for 3.
repeat the hammer on pattern when you play the F after the C.
when playing the G chord, the last beat of the measure you should switch to Gsus (only for a beat).

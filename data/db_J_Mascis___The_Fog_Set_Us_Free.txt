J Mascis and The Fog - Set Us Free
From the album 'Free So Free' (2002)

Standard tuning, capo on 9th fret

---

This tab is based on what I could see from a video of it being played live/some fancy
sound-slowing software/oldschool listening for notes, so it's got to be fairly accurate.

Due to the way the guitar parts seem to have been recorded with like 2-3 overdubs and/or
certain effects, i've changed the tab so it's more like how it's played live (with one
guitar). That way the switches between the rhythm and the lead parts/small fills won't
seem so painfully obvious when you're playing along.

(Edit a few years later after tabbing this - turns out, the palm mutes aren't palm mutes! J himself has
said he just flat out didn't know how to do them until ~2010, and said he'd just been doing
fretting hand mutes all his life. So... I don't know, ignore the palm mutes and make them left hand
mutes if you strive for total accuracy. I still think they were intended as, and sound better, as palm mutes.)

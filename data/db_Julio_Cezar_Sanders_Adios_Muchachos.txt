Lyrics
------

A man about 50/60 years old, is seriously ill and is saying goodbye to his 
group of friends (barra de amigos). All the song is recalling the good times 
gone-by. He especially remembers his mother (santa viejita) and his little 
fiance&#1081;, both dead and whom he misses without finding comfort. It is, 
definitively, a sad quite melancholic goodbye.

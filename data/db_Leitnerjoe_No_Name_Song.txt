If you dont know how to slide with a harmonic here is how:
-Play a harmonic by playing the string while hovering your finger on the string above the fret you
 desire to make a harminic on
-While the harmonic is still ringing out press down on the string and slide the the desired fret

This will only work on a fretless bass as the frets on a regular bass stop the note from ringing 
out

MANO NEGRA -- Paris La Nuit
Track 14 on the album "King of Bongo" from 1991, also released as a CD-single the same year.
Words and music by Manu(el) Chao.

Essentially, this can be considered the same song as "Ronde de Nuit" from the 1988 album "Patchanka".
Both versions are fetaured on the 1991 CD-single.
While "Ronde de Nuit" is a fast-paced rock song, "Paris La Nuit" is a much slower version with a waltz-style
rhythm (3/4 time)
The chords are quite different as well, so I figured it would be worth tabbing the song.
Lyrics are mostly identical. However, some of the latter verses from "Ronde de Nuit" are omitted (because
you can fit in a lot more words in a faster song, I guess :-)

Enjoy.

artist - o' god the white whale
song - brand new song
no capo
Drop B tuning (B F# B E G# C#)

fantastic song from a fantastic but sadly short-lived screamo band, the first song off their only release, the
ep "never knows best".

there are two guitars in this song, but this tab is only for guitar 1, which according to one of the
guitarists from the band (thanks again for the help travis!) was a baritone guitar played in drop A with a 
capo on fret 2. because i don't own a baritone, i've tabbed this in drop B, which is the equivalent of drop A
with a capo on 2.

he also told me the second guitar part (panned right) was likely to be either standard or drop D, and i
planned to figure out the part for that second guitar but found it too hard to pick out, especially during
the more chord-y parts of the song. so i came up with this one-guitar version based on the right guitar part 
which i think sounds good if you're playing by yourself (even though i'm not sure all the notes are right).

while figuring out this tab, i also accidentally managed to figure out the intro to "ends. endings." so i
might figure out the rest of that song and post it too, although i might just post the intro if i can't
manage to get the whole song.

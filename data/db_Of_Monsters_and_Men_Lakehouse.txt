Here is Lakehouse by Of Monsters and Men. This is the original album version - the one
on the latest US release is arranged differently for some reason and misses the 'tallest
man I've ever seen afloat' verse. If you want to learn to play that version you can use 
this tab and just figure out the differences. In my opinion this is the better of the 
two versions.

This is also the way they play the song live.

-Paul (please feel free to contact me with changes etc. at paul.j.gregory@gmail.com)

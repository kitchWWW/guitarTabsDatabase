Notes:
I'd like to say that I'm POSITIVE there are doubles in the bass drum in some 
parts of the song. If you're going to argue this, please make sure you've 
listen to the song carefully (extra bass helps). One more thing is in the 
video it looks like he's hitting 2 crashes at once, I wasn't sure (too hard to 
tell) so I left it at one. 
Here is the legend I use throughout the song:

>  =  Accent
C  =  Crash cymbals; 1 for splash, 2 for low, 3 for normal, 4 for high, 5 for Chinese
R  =  Ride cymbal; "x" for normal, "O" for bell
H  =  High Hats; "x" for closed, "X" for semi-open, "O" for open
T  =  Tom
T2 =  Tom-Tom
S  =  Snare; "o" for normal, "x" for rim-shot
F  =  Floor tom
B  =  Bass drum

One of my favourite Parkway songs, just thought I would re-tab this song as most of the
out there are

only partly ot mostly correct. Mostly they feature most of the song right, then some of
is slightly out

of whack.

Thanks to Benny, for submitting the original tab, I also better reference where he got
from too, - Juz or

someone. Cheers Boys

Song is in Drop B. (B,F#,B,E,G#,C#) or (B,Gb,B,E,Ab,Db) Whatever floats your boat

Intro - Is distorted, contrary to popular belief. However, Jeff plays it clean live, but
the recording,

it is distorted.
Also it is using a chorus effect over the top. (If you are cheap, like me, and only have
practice amp and

no pedal, and you have a three-way toggle on your guitar, set it to the neck 
pickup, and have the volume

around 3-5 on the guitar, and full tone. when you want full distortion, flick it back to
bridge pick-

up. Havent tried this with a stratocaster, but it should be similar, providing you 
have a humbucker for

your bridge pick-up).


Something like this......

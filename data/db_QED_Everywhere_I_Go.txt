Everywhere I Go - QED (other releases are by Jenny Morris, the lead singer).
From 1984's "Animal Magic".

Notes: you can follow the vocal in the chorus and play F, F6, Fmaj7 if you want to emulate the lead line.
Note that the recording is slightly sharp. Also, the Bm - Am - Em sequence at the end of each chorus is power
chords, but I think minor chords work well, followed by the E major as played by the synth. Jenny Morris' solo
version has a different chord progression in the chorus. Solo chords are the same as the chorus.

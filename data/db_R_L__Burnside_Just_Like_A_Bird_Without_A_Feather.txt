I tried to tab this: "https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=N06HisNLmTw" and i compared it to the tabs of 
Samuel L. Jackson's in "Black Snake Moan". Its almost original. 
Look this Tutorial: 
"https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LlHUNCJAF9k&t=85s" for the slide (which is optional) and the Hammer on

when he sings he doesn't play anything but you can use the muted lower E string for a metronome
(which also makes a cool sound). 
Don't get frustrated about the Tabs by the look, the Tutorial with the Hammer on's makes it a lot easier 
to see what it's meant to be played.

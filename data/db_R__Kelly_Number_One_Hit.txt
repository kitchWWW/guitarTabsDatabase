The original song is played in piano, this approximates it in guitar. 

This meloday in the introduction is played 2 times in the beginning of the song.
| F  (6st fret B string) is played while chording the C#M7
| Eb (4th Fret B string) played while chording AbM7
| C   last note (5th fret G string) is sometime played live 

This pattern is repeated in the entire song
| C#M7    = x 4 6 5 6 4
| AbM7    = 4 x 5 5 4 x
| C# (4th fret A string) is used a the bass for the C#M7
| Ab (4th fret E sting) is used as the bass for the AbM7 chord
| h  hammer on
| x  dead/muted note

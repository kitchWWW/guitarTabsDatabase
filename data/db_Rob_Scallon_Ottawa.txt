Ottawa                                      
by Rob Scallon



Very simple song and really good for beginners to fingerpicking. The song is in a 
strange tuning though.

Here's how to tune the guitar
-Low E string goes up a half step to F
-D string goes down a step to C
-and B string goes up a half step to C

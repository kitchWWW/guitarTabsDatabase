Getting back into Scapegoat Wax with the drop of some new singles from Marty James and thought I might update
some of the songs here.  This is a very easy and catchy song with some solid vocal harmonies and really
benefits from major 7th chords to add a little more depth since there are only three chords at play.  Chord
might look tough to a beginner but you can use the same barre chord shape and just move it around the neck for
simplicity.

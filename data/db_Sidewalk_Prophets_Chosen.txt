IMPORTANT PERFORMANCE NOTES: 1) The Intro is a keyboard program arranged for Guitar 2 and is carried into 
                                all three verses, chorus and the Interlude with additional keyboard parts 
                                arranged for Guitar 2 also 

                             2) Guitar 1 has to tackle some VERY FAST alternate picking throughout the 
                                entire song. Whoever plays Guitar 1 has his hands full

                             3) The song as recorded is a bit difficult because of the swift and unrelenting
                                picking that Guitar 1 has to perform but the song can easily be played w/o
                                the picking and simply strummed using the chords in ( ).

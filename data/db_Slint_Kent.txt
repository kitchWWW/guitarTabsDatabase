 I am not sure exactly how Brian plays this part. It's actually all harmonics, even the C note on the 10th
 fret of the D string which is not really possible to get with natural harmonics. He might play this in
 a different tuning. You can also kinda hear the pitch fluctuate in between harmonics so he might just be
 getting the 12th fret on D string harmonic and then lowering and raising his whammy bar to get the different
 notes. This is the easiest way to play it in standard tuning, the only problem is you can't get the C note 
 natural harmonic.

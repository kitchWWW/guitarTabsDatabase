-I'm going to need to make a lot of notes for this one since it's so hard to tab it out
by note with its numerous semitones and random patterns. First of all, in the studio
Bill's guitar was obviously off tune. To get on the exact pitch, Tune your D, G, and 
E strings up one semitone and tune your A string down one semitone. Then, you should be
to play everything on this tab...

These are some keys to understand the transcription:
+  means thumb(slap)
v  means popping
/  means slide up
\  means slide down
-X- means ghost note(done by pressing the string without touching the fretboard)
h  means hammer
p  means pull

So take a look at this transcription,but take into consideration that I haven't
transcribed here all the (continuos)variation that White do.
Enjoy yourself,play a bass(or something else!)!!!

Note on tuning: Live footage on YouTube shows that Rachel Haden plays this in standard tuning, so that
                is how I transcribed it.  I learned the song in Drop-D tuning, and I think it's a little
                easier to play that way.  So, if you want, tune the low string to D and then change 1 to 3,
                3 to 5, and 5 to the open A string.

I wrote this out a little while ago.
This was transcribed all by ear so it may not be 100% correct but it sounds correct to me... better than
nothing, I guess lol

Adam Demirjian, the guy behind most of The Brave Little Abacus said that most of Just Got Back and Okumay
were done in either D standard/whole step down (DGCFAD) or a variant of it (DGCFGC). 
Because of this, and since the only difference between those two tunings is the highest two strings on a 
normal guitar, I'm assuming that the bass is tuned to DGCF (one whole step down).

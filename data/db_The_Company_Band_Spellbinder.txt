The Company Band has quickly made it's way to one of my all time favorite bands, and the lack of love for them
on UG is a little sad to me! So, enjoy this tab and let me know of any corrections either through the UG 
website, or by email: slowermrborat@live.com

A lot of the chord progressions could be played in first position, but personally, I enjoy the jumps from 2nd
to 3rd, and sometimes back to 1st. It brings a little bit of variety and extra enjoyment when playing this fun
song.

Thanks all, enjoy.

Spellbinder (with Solo)
The Company Band
E Standard Tuning

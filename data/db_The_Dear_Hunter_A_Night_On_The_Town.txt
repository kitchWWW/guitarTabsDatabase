probably know, some parts feature very little guitar and are very bass / piano heavy. Therefore, choose
a strumming pattern that sounds good to you. I have tried to mark the timings of the chord changes within
the tab where possible. I've also tabbed out any prominent riffs / motifs, which can be played instead of
the chords. I've included lyrics and timings in the song where appropriate.

Notes in () are optional, and notes in [] are to let ring. I've also included the names of the chords 
if you'd prefer to play them in a different position.

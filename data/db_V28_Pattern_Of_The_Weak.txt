Dropped B Tuning

>  = play note with added emphasis
+  = pinch harmonics (only used on very first note of song)
\  = slide down to lower note
h  = hammer on
p  = pull off
.  = palm mute
*  = tremolo
() = note(s) held from previous measure (also cont'd use of tremolo where noted)
/\ = triplets (they'll be above the notes)

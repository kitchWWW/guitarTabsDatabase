.  - palm mute           /  - slide up to
\  - slide down to       ~  - vibrato
h  - hammer on           b  - Bend
p  - pull off            Suffixes for bend
t  - tap                 f  - full bend    h - half bend
ph - pinched harmonic    q  - quarter bend t - tap bend
*  - see comment         ^  - Hold bend    r release bend
x  - Stuccatto           ~  - vibrato bend
,  - slight palm mute    () - ghost note, sustained note
"  - tremolo note        &lt;> - Trill

Tunning - whole step (D etc.)

Note: This is my favourite song from the album (that and Cold Demons). It's 
not actually that hard to play (all riffs are played fairly fast) but there 
are loads of different fills and variations, which is quite common with 
Vader. All the fast picking parts are not accurate but listen to the song 
for lengths and timings. Feel free to Email me if you have any questions. 
Enjoy

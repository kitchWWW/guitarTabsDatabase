NOTE: IMPORTANT. PLEASE READ THIS BEFORE ATTEMPTING THIS TAB.

When you see this: -0- , it means you have to strum the strings using nails to create the sound as
                                   -0-
                                   -0-
                                   -0-
                                   -0-
                                   ---
heard in the recording. I can't explain it in words, but listen to the song and
you will know what I mean. I cannot tab this out exactly like the recording as it
is too random, as Im figuring out the tab (and listening to Monte repeatedly,)  I
mostly did it with "feel", so do the same too, don't follow this tab strictly as
it is, because as I said, Zee strums the strings randomly throughout the song.
However, there is a pattern to the song, especially in the verse, after every bass
note, Zee strums the rest of the strings, open.

HOW TO PLAY HARMONICS: It is like barring all the strings at the fret only you DO
NOT PRESS THEM, instead, you just 'touch' the strings lightly, near to the right
'metal bar' of the fret to achieve a high note.

I hope I did not make this tab seem confusing because it is really not difficult
to play the song once you get the rhythm and the pattern of the song. So I suggest
you to listen to the song till you are able to recognise the pattern and the
rhythm, then play together with the song. You can also watch Zee's video here
http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WdXDgaEgaJ8 to get a rough idea of how she strums/
plucks. Monte is the last of the 4 songs in the video, it starts at around 07:42.

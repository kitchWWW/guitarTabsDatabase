I couldn't find any bass tabs for this song, so I decided to figure it out for myself and
then make some myself.

I don't know if it's 100% accurate (I have my doubts about the A:|-2-2-2-2-2-2-4-0- and 
E:|-5-5-5-5-5-5-5-0- parts), but this is how I play "Not An Addict" (If anyone has any
other suggestions please mail them to me).

Also the A:|-14- means fourteen and all the other ones like A:|-5-5-5-5-5-5-5555- are meant
to demonstrate the rythm of the song, I know it's looks a bit distracting.

I have tabbed the entire song so just play along with the song and you should do fine.

Hope you enjoy it!
